  About

Hi! My name's Nate, and I'm an internet addict. They say acceptance is the first step on the road to recovery. I love creating new ways of communicating and understanding information, and I've been learning and building on the web for about as long as it's existed as a publicly accessible resource. I first heard the sound of a modem connecting to the internet back in 1989 when I was about six years old, and I've been hooked ever since.

I'm currently focusing on deepening my knowledge of geographical information systems, bidirectional real time communication channels (primarily WebSocket), and new kinds of web server software like Node.js to see if I can play a part in shaping the next generation of web applications.

I've been living in Portland, Oregon for close to three years and I'm thoroughly enjoying the good coffee, beer, books, and the blossoming technology industry.

If you need to get in touch with me I'm active on twitter and IRC (you can sometimes find me at #pdxtech on irc.freenode.net as @ngoldman) and I also check my email regularly. If I don't respond right away, I'm probably buried under a pile of work, but I'll get back to you as soon as I can. Thanks for visiting!
